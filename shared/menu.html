Genesis Theatre

Genesis Theatre

was an amateur theatre group from the late 70's and early 80's. This site is dedicated to keeping the memory of such a great group alive.

While we are in touch with most of the main protagonists we would like to contact anybody who had an involvement with Genesis Theatre Group, however brief, during those magic years.

If you are one of those people, or know one of them, please contact us by clicking here. We would love to include a short theatrical biography for as many members as we can.
